Engineering Consultants

NS Holdings Engineering Pvt Ltd (NSHE) is a multi-disciplinary consulting company established in 2014 as a partnership and subsequently incorporated as a private limited liability company. NSHE is a member of The Association of Consulting Engineers Sri Lanka (ACESL) affiliated to the International Federation of Consulting Engineers (FIDIC), and the Ceylon Chamber of Commerce Sri Lanka.


The company has been in operation for over 10 years with a track record of many successfully completed projects in Sri Lanka as well as in the Asian region such as Maldives, India, Bangladesh, and African region such as Uganda, Timor, Uzbekistan & etc.


NSHE offers services from project concept to completion in a wide range of sectors and, a comprehensive range of skills offered by NSHE as an integrated part of consultancy services include preliminary investigations, feasibility studies, environmental assessments, development planning, detailed designs, contract documentation, project management, construction supervision, technical assistance, institutional strengthening and training.
